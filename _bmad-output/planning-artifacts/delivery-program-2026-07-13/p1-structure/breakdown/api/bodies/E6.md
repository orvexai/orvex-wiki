## 🗺️ Epic — Chat History & Chat-History Import

External import is one of the three intake routes into the Librarian's staging area (Brief §1) — the one-off backfill of your ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/Grok history, distilled through the same propose-and-confirm gate. This epic owns **Chat History**: the per-user Conversation store (metadata/turns in Postgres, bodies + raw Archives in S3, encrypted), provider/date browse, cross-links to distilled wiki/Memory items, and retention controls (Archives auto-delete 30d post-import default; keep-forever/delete-now; per-conversation/provider/all hard-delete incl. search-index entries via knowledge purge events; account-deletion extends the central purge saga — FR-SA18). It owns **Chat-History Import**: the guided per-provider wizard (content-managed instructions, resumable pending-import state), multi-GB Archive upload to S3, Time Window, and per-provider parse adapters (ChatGPT `current_node` active-branch walk, Claude `conversations.json`, then Gemini/Grok/Copilot at Beta) — with the parse pipeline executing as a **central workflow** calling studio-api step-APIs (FR-SA19). And it owns **paste-prompt quick import** (FR-SA20).

Free fills Chat History; £7/trial additionally feeds the Curator (E5). Search over Conversations is knowledge's chat-history corpus (delegated).

**Why (cites):** FR-SA18–20 (PRD `85qj2wwU2L`); Brief §1 "External import … the Phase-2 Chat-History Import, reusing the Librarian pipeline as bulk backfill."
