**Audit: already implemented** (evidence in §4) — this story is VERIFY + harden: prove the behaviour via the named DoD test + adversarial review; do not rebuild.

**As** the verifier, **I want** bounded, Redis-cached introspection whose result feeds the effective-scope gate, with a fixed maximum staleness wall independent of introspection reachability, **so that** a scope reduction or revocation takes effect no later than the cache TTL and a DoS on the introspect path can never keep a token alive (FR-L2, FR-L3; Arch A-VERIFY-IMPL step 5).

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestIntrospectionStalenessWall` (unit, in `pkg/auth`, driven through `Verify` against a fake introspect server) asserting that past the hard staleness wall with identity unreachable the verifier **fails closed**, and that a scope reduction reflected in a fresh introspection result narrows the effective scope. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2092); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
