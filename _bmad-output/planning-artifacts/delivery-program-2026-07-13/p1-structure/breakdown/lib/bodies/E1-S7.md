## 🎯 Story

**As** a satellite calling the engine or knowledge, **I want** the api-key credential helper (`Authorization: Bearer`) with rotation support and no shared `APP_SECRET`, **so that** outbound service calls carry the engine's AGPL-clean api-key (engine FR-11) uniformly and key rotation is a supported, non-breaking operation (FR-L8; Arch §5 — `APIKeyCredential.Apply` already hardcodes `Authorization: Bearer`).

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestAPIKeyCredentialRotation` (unit, in `pkg/auth`, driving the credential through the outbound client transport) asserting it stamps `Authorization: Bearer <key>`, supports mid-flight rotation to a new key without dropping in-flight requests, and that no shared `APP_SECRET` exists anywhere in the module (ADR-S6 stays retired). *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2092); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
