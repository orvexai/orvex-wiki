## 🧭 Feature Area

`pkg/events` is the family's single CloudEvents 1.0 seam: envelope construct/parse/validate (structured + binary mode), the generated event-type constants and payload types for the `wiki.*` / `clerk.*` / identity-lifecycle catalog, and the publisher that emits onto the Kafka-backed `studio-spine` Broker (PRD F2 / FR-L9–L11). Per Arch A-CELL Rule 6 it is *also* the shared envelope helper that stamps the `orvexcell` extension attribute and makes every consumer path **fail closed on a cell mismatch** — "the single most cell-load-bearing responsibility `pkg/events` owns." No hand-rolled envelope parsing may exist anywhere else in the family.

Why it exists: without one envelope seam, each satellite hand-rolls CloudEvents parsing and cell-scoping — and a foreign-cell event or a malformed envelope becomes a silent cross-tenant leak instead of a typed rejection. Kafka-first (USER 2026-07-05): publisher + consumers are launch-line, not deferred.
