## 🎯 Story

**As** a caller of any lib client, **I want** narrow-never-widen ACL/export typing (no failure, empty, or timeout can read as "allow all") plus uniform client discipline — per-call deadlines to published budgets, bounded retries with backoff, circuit-breaker hooks, auto-obs — **so that** callers are structurally forced into the engine-down-⇒-fail-closed invariant and no unbounded retry loop exists (FR-L13, FR-L14; Arch A-CLIENTS).

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestACLNarrowNeverWidens` (unit, in `pkg/engineclient`, driving `ACLNarrow` through failure/empty/timeout paths) asserting `(allowSet, error)` where a nil allowSet with nil error is impossible and an empty allowSet never means "all", plus that every method enforces a deadline and bounded retries. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2092); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
