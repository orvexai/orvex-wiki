## 📖 Epic — Projection store, multi-source model & vendor seam

The read-optimized replica of any Studio entity (D-S12) lives here: a **single Postgres store** (JSONB projection + range-partitioned journal append tables, RLS fail-closed — Mongo struck), a **Turbopuffer** hybrid keyword+vector index (committed, D-S9) behind a **vendor-neutral store-adapter seam** (A3, NFR-K7), and the **source-adapter contract** (A4) that lets any publisher register without pipeline changes. This is the storage foundation every other epic writes to and reads from.

Why it exists: the PRD's load-bearing invariant is that chunk **text never lives in Postgres** (it lives in Turbopuffer + Redis) — that is what keeps a 250k-tenant cell under the ~1 TB backup/RTO breaker (A2). The multi-source document model (FR-K7) is what lets wiki ship first and Studio corpora (E10) join without schema breaks. The adapter seam keeps a store swap a config change, proven by a conformance suite that runs against a `memindex` reference in CI (NFR-K7).
