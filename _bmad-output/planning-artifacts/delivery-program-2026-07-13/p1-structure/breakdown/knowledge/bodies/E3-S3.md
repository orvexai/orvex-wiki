As the speed layer I want a Redis read-path cache with event-driven tag eviction so that hot queries are blindingly fast — while never caching an allow-decision and never leaking across tenants.

**Definition of Done:** `TestReadPathCacheEvictionAndNoCachedAllow` (integration, `internal/cache`+indexer) — query-embedding/candidate/doc/related caches serve hits, the indexer evicts affected tag-keyed entries as its FIRST action on any content/permission/purge event, and no allow/deny decision or accessible-ID set is ever cached. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2099); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
