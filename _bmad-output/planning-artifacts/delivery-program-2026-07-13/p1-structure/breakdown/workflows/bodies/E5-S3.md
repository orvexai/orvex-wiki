## 🎯 Story

**As a** workflows platform engineer
**I want** personal signups provisioned as a **user-keyed tenant** (`user:<clerkUserId>`, minting NO Clerk org) through the same `ProvisionWorkflow`, with the workflow-ID scheme generalized to the polymorphic tenant ID {user|org}
**so that** a solo user gets a full wiki workspace + entitlements without incurring Clerk per-org MAU cost (D-S17), and org provisioning stays Teams-only.

**Definition of Done:** one named test `TestPersonalTenantProvisionsNoOrg` — workflow-test-env layer: a personal signal provisions a `user:<id>` tenant with a full workspace + Free entitlement and mints no Clerk org; an org signal still mints a real org
