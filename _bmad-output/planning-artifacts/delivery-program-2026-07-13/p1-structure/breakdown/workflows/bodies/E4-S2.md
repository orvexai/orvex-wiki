## 🎯 Story

**As a** workflows platform engineer
**I want** `CELL_ID` (required in prod, distinct from `CLUSTER_NAME`) echoed by `/healthz` alongside `status`, and the `orvexcell` CloudEvent extension stamped by the shared envelope helper on every publish with consumers failing closed on mismatch
**so that** the tenant-lifecycle domain honours the day-1 cell contract (rules 4 + 6) it lacks today.
