## 🎯 Story

**Audit: already implemented** (evidence in §4) — this story is VERIFY + harden: prove the behaviour via the named DoD test + adversarial review; do not rebuild.
As every OpenAPI seam file, I want the shared `openapi/_components/` — one error envelope, one pagination shape, and the two auth schemes (bearer referencing the F5 principal/token-scope schemas; workload-identity mTLS/SPIFFE with `x-rejects-bearer: true`) — so that the authorization vocabulary and error shape cannot fork per satellite.

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestComponentsSingleSourceOfAuth` — a CI-layer test asserting, through `validate.yaml`, that every `openapi/*.yaml` and `openapi/internal/*.yaml` references the shared `_components` auth scheme by `$ref` (bearer for public, workload-identity for internal) and inlines none. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2091); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
