## 🗺️ Feature area

The Phase-2 curation surfaces are where the Librarian loop becomes visible to the user: import your ChatGPT/Claude history, browse your chat corpus, and work the Curation Queue where AI proposals become committed knowledge only on your recorded approval. Grounded in the Phase-2 PRD (FR-39–70), this epic delivers Demo World (the persistent banner, guided first-run, the visible demo-mode enrichment citations that are the aha mechanic, and atomic graduation with fork keep/discard), the Chat-History Import wizard (per-provider content-managed instructions, honest trap-naming, resumable multi-GB upload, time-window picker, async progress), the free first-class Chat History surface (browse, instant knowledge-backed search, faithful conversation render, retention controls), and the Curator desk + Curation Queue (grouped proposals, per-group approve only — no global approve-all, provenance, diff previews, 100+ scale). It exists because "nothing renders as landed without the recorded approval event" (G3) is the product's trust spine, and these are the surfaces where it is most load-bearing.
