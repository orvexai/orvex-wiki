## 🗺️ Feature area

The wiki-consumption, connection, and money surfaces. Grounded in Phase-2 PRD FR-59–69 and the pricing/trial decisions (D-S19/D-S20/D-S23), this epic delivers Your Wiki (the single omnibox fusing free instant search and £7 Ask with inline citations, the native BFF-fetched Studio-styled read pane, ≤150ms wiki hover cards, and wiki-framed activity with magic-link "Edit in wiki ↗" handoff), Connections & MCP setup (one-time-secret display the SPA never stores, per-connection write-mode switching, guided setup for the Five AIs' MCP clients), and Pricing/Billing/Trial (GBP-only VAT-inclusive public pricing, Stripe Checkout/Portal redirects, the single SSE entitlement channel flipping the paywall <60s without reload, the 7-day card-required trial, and the 14-day-grace auto-downgrade dunning truth). It exists to close the "un-lock-in layer" story (Your Wiki + MCP capture) and the business model (billing) while holding the no-secrets / no-custom-billing-UI / read-only-wiki non-goals.
