## 🗺️ Feature area

Discover and the Skill Viewer/Builder are the marketplace-and-editing core — the "most amazing prompt editor in the world" bar the brief raises. Grounded in MA2/MA3, this epic delivers the marketplace home with skeleton→outcome-first cards and results-as-you-type search (perceived-latency ownership, ≤300ms p95 warm surfaced), the anon wall-free variant, the Skill Viewer that previews typed Items in plain language with per-block provenance and declared injection slots, and the Base+Delta Builder where fork/edit never mutates Base, the deterministic weave/render completes client-side <50ms offline, autosave shows Saving…→Saved with retry, and two-device concurrency renders last-write-wins with a non-destructive "updated to vN" banner. It exists because this is the substrate the brief says must be "substantially improved, not inherited" from the POC — the editor that teaches non-technical users what a good prompt is.
