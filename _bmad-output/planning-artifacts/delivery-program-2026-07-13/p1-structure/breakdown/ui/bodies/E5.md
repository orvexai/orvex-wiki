## 🗺️ Feature area

Improve-with-AI and the outbound surfaces are the payoff: the AI improves your prompt, you accept the diff, and you launch it into any of the Five AIs. Grounded in MB2/`DRP67l1a6q` and MB1/FR-62/63, this epic delivers the Improve panel with its five result states and non-optimistic Apply (the Delta PATCH must succeed before "✓ Applied"), the capture-back seam (Save to Memory after Apply, user's own value, consent-gated), five-AI export with the Tier-1 clipboard-blocked fallback that never degrades, share permalinks + prompt-via-us launch with the Enrich-this-prompt tick (ticked by default for entitled users, silent, never persisted), and Library/Collections. It exists to carry the draft-vs-committed invariant at its sharpest (G3) and the outbound honesty rules (enrichment never persists; Copy is Tier-1 everywhere; SM-C3 = 0 private-leak on every outbound surface).
