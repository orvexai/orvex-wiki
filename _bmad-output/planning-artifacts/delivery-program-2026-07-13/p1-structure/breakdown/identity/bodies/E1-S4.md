## 🎯 Story

As the **platform security owner**, I want fail-closed grace with a **hard wall-clock staleness bound**, a fresh-introspection requirement for high-privilege ops, and a push-revocation channel, so that an attacker cannot DoS the introspect path to keep a revoked token alive.

**Definition of Done:** `TestFailClosedHardBound` (integration layer) — through `lib/auth`, once satellite-cached introspection exceeds the fixed max staleness (independent of introspection reachability) the satellite fails closed; a high-privilege op requires a fresh, no-grace introspection; a push-revocation broadcast lands a token on the satellite deny-list.
