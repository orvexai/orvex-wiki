## 🎯 Feature area — Anti-Sybil / free-tier abuse controls

The free tier (200 pages · 1 GiB · 2,000 files · 25 members, + a lifetime AI-action trial) is farmable: one actor minting thousands of PERSONAL free tenants for free hosting, and resetting the AI trial by re-minting tenants. Identity is the **durable enforcement point** — every principal flows through the tenant-to-cell registry write, and controls must apply across BOTH IdP paths (Clerk + Keycloak). The strategy is **defense-in-depth, gate the resource not the account**: the flagship structural move is to **bind the lifetime AI-trial ledger to a verified principal that outlives tenants** (deleting/re-creating a tenant cannot reset it); plus a per-principal live-tenant cap, progressive-trust tiers + staged provisioning, table-stakes edge controls on every personal signup, and a fused risk score firing heavy step-up (phone/card) **only on suspicion** — never on the 100k happy path.

Why it exists: Brief §"Delivery Doctrine"/Scope P2 "anti-Sybil anchor" + the anti-Sybil canon page (CYHyoUd7J1, DRAFT options — final anchor strength is a PO decision pinned by ENG-2101). Targets the PERSONAL free-signup path (org tenants carry paid seats). GDPR-proportional, EU-pinned.
