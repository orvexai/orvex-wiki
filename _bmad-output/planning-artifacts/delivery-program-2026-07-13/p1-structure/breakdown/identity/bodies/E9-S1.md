## 🎯 Story

As the **platform**, I want a `verified_principal` entity that owns the lifetime AI-trial ledger (keyed by IdP `user_id`/`sub` plus a normalized-email HMAC), so that deleting and re-creating a tenant cannot reset the trial — the one structural change that kills the tenant-reminting reset.

**Definition of Done:** `TestTrialLedgerSurvivesTenantReset` (integration layer) — through the AI-action authorization path, the `verified_principal` owns `ai_trial_actions_remaining`, the metering call decrements the **principal** (never a tenant counter), and a new personal tenant copies the residual and never resets it.
