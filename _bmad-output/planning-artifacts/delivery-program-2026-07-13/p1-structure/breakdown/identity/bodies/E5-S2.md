## 🎯 Story

As the **platform**, I want `deprovision(delete)` to be **two-phase** — identity refuses immediate hard-purge and only soft-disables; the irreversible purge is gated behind the workflows grace timer + out-of-band confirmation — so that a destructive call cannot irrevocably purge without a safety window.

**Definition of Done:** `TestDeprovisionTwoPhase` (integration layer) — through `/api/clerk/deprovision {mode=delete}`, identity soft-disables immediately and does NOT purge; purge occurs only after the workflows grace timer + out-of-band confirmation; every delete is rate-limited and alerted out-of-band, and the purge emits a purge event.
