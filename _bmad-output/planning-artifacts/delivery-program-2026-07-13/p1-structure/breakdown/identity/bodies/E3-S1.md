## 🎯 Story

**Audit: already implemented** (evidence in §4) — this story is VERIFY + harden: prove the behaviour via the named DoD test + adversarial review; do not rebuild.

As the **tenant authority**, I want a registry that records per tenant its `idp` and a **polymorphic `{user|org}` mapping** with opaque, non-derivable IDs, so that a personal user is a user-keyed tenant with no Clerk org and a Teams tenant is a Clerk org, resolved the same way.

**Definition of Done:** `TestPolymorphicTenantMapping` (integration layer) — through the registry, a Clerk user resolves to a user-keyed tenant (`org_or_realm` empty), a Clerk org resolves to an org-tenant, a Keycloak realm resolves to its tenant, and tenant IDs are opaque (never derivable from `sub`/email/realm).
