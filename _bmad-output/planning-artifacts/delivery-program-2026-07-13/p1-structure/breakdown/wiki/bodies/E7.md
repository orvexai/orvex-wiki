## 🗺️ Feature area

This epic turns the deliberately-vanilla single-workspace engine into a safe multi-tenant cell. It delivers the **CLOUD-clean boot** (FR-W20 — `app.module.ts` must not `process.exit(1)` when `CLOUD=true` and the absent `ee/ee.module` fails to load; nothing on the multi-tenant hot path may depend on `ee/`) with the existing `domain.middleware.ts` `isCloud()` → `split('.')[0]` → `workspaceRepo.findByHostname` resolver (label-0 tenant, unchanged under D-S27 `{tenant}.wiki.{cell}`) plus a **soft, defence-in-depth label-2 cell assertion that MUST no-op under the `solo` sentinel** so self-hosted boots still work; the **transaction-scoped fail-closed RLS** backstop (FR-W8 — `set_config('app.workspace_id',$1,true)` because pgBouncer runs transaction-pooling; a session-level `SET` leaks across tenants); the **polymorphic user-or-org tenancy** (A-TENANCY / D-S17 — a personal user gets *no* Clerk org, a user-keyed tenant with full workspace + entitlements; Teams get a real Clerk org; the personal→Teams **upgrade-pass** re-keys the tenant in place carrying data + entitlements; global uniqueness of `tenant_id`/hostname delegates to the identity registry); and **severing the upstream Stripe seat-sync** from AGPL core (FR-W21 / A-BOUNDARY — neutralize `STRIPE_SEATS_SYNC`/`BILLING_QUEUE` to emit a `billing.*` event, retire the in-engine billing migration, no Stripe secret in the engine set).

Why it exists: G2 (airtight multi-tenant safety, cross-tenant bytes = 0) and A-BOUNDARY (AGPL cleanliness before source publication).
