## 🎯 Epic — Search, AI & Admin Namespaces

Domain-pure routing for the non-wiki peers (D-S16). The **search** namespace routes to `orvex-studio-knowledge` (`keyword/semantic/hybrid/related/duplicates/attachment-search`) with a `--cached` local-FTS5 fallback that preserves FTS sanitisation and the superseded/archived default-exclusion. The **ai** namespace routes to `orvex-studio-ai` (`ask/chat/inline/image/models/cost`), returning the cited-ask shape `{answer,citations,unanswered,gapNote,confidence,followups}` and surfacing `BUDGET_EXCEEDED`/`MODEL_UNAVAILABLE`. The **admin** namespace fans out per finding F-E: `workspace(info/members/invites/integrations/entitlements)` + `user` to wiki-api/identity, entitlement **state** read from the knowledge FR-K20 projection (fed by `billing.entitlement.changed`, never a CLI-facing billing host), and `reindex/reembed/events(settings|connections|log)` to knowledge events-admin. Neither search nor ask ever touches the engine. [Source: PRD R4AOVBLST7 §F2 FR-CLI5 / §F4 FR-CLI11; Architecture pf10XC2Qjz §Data flow admin routing]

Why it exists: these namespaces prove the "wiki is one namespace among peers" thesis and exercise the cross-service RAG/ask surfaces (search/ai/knowledge-sync) that the six-surface proving protocol requires — while keeping billing correctly absent from the dependency set.
