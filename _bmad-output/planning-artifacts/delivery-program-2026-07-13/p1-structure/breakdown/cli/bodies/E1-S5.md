## 🎯 Story

**Audit: already implemented** (evidence in §4) — this story is VERIFY + harden: prove the behaviour via the named DoD test + adversarial review; do not rebuild.

As a **direct-to-cell agent**, I want the transport to treat a 421 cell-mismatch as a typed retriable-after-re-resolve condition — re-resolving the tenant-to-cell mapping via one authoritative registry read, re-pointing the host, cold-starting any cursor bound to the old events-host, then retrying once — so that a tenant-move or mis-set cell never hard-fails or lands writes on the wrong cell.

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestTransport421ReResolvesAndRetriesOnce` — a transport-layer test asserting, through `Do(service, req)` against a stub returning 421 then 200, that the transport performs exactly one registry re-resolve, re-points the host, retries once, and surfaces a typed `CELL_MISMATCH` if resolution fails. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2105); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
