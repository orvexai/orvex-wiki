## 🎯 Story

As a **wiki agent**, I want `space`, `comment`, `label`, and `attach` verbs plus binary attachment upload/download routed to orvex-wiki-api, so that spaces/comments/labels CRUD and binary attachment I/O work through the facade — with attachment *search* being the one exception that routes to knowledge.

**Definition of Done:** ONE named test `TestSpaceCommentLabelAttachRouting` — a wiki-client test asserting, through the space/comment/label/attach commands, that CRUD + binary upload/download target wiki-api, and that `attachment-search` alone routes to knowledge. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2105); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*
