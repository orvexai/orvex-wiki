## 🧭 Epic — Facts, prime & retrieval: the cheap-warm-start layer

The payoff surface: every session starts warm. This epic delivers persistent tenant/agent-scoped
Remembered Facts (`remember`/`recall`/`forget`) with injection caps and explicit elision banners,
the `prime` call in compact and full modes with a fixed content contract and a ≤300-token compact
budget, and hybrid retrieval (`ranking`) that fuses the knowledge service's semantic leg with
tsvector keyword search, graph adjacency, and recency/importance — with **zero LLM calls on any
hot path** (NFR-MEM1's committed invariant). The retrieval spike closes the epic: it validates the
provisional SLOs on the real AD-3 topology and lights the fast-path index-scan tier (exact-key
recall, pinned-only prime-compact).

Why it exists: UJ-1 — cold start becomes warm start, the session's first useful action within one
tool call. AD-3 rides the semantic leg on knowledge/Turbopuffer (no service-local vector store);
AD-13 fixes the prime content contract and the elision banner envelope. The retrieval spike is a
GA sequencing gate, not a deferral, and is the ADR-0014-carve-out trigger if the budget fails.
