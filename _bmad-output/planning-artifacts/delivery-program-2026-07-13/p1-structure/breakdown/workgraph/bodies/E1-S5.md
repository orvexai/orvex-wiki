### 🎯 Story
As a **coordinating agent** I want **every work item to carry a server-issued per-tenant short id (`wg-` + base32-crockford of a per-tenant sequence)** so that **I can reference items in notes and across surfaces with a stable human-typeable handle, without beads' decentralized hash-ladder machinery**.

**Definition of Done:** one named test `TestShortIdUniquePerTenant` (store/integration layer) asserts short ids are unique on `(tenant, short_id)`, monotonic per tenant, and parse identically across the shared grammar. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2095); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.*

- [ ] **AC2** — Given the same sequence position in tenant A and tenant B, When both create items, Then the short ids may collide across tenants but never within a tenant. *assert: `(A, wg-1)` and `(B, wg-1)` coexist; `(A, wg-1)` twice is rejected.* [Source: AD-6]
