## 🎯 Story

As a durable loop, I want caller authority carried as an identity-minted, narrowly-scoped delegation token (loop-appropriate TTL) or an opaque handle ai resolves at tool-exec time — never a raw caller token in workflow inputs — so that Temporal history and console's workflow pane never hold token material and a minutes-lived session token can't expire mid-loop (FR-AI21, D-AI10).

**Definition of Done:** one named test `TestNoRawTokenInWorkflowInputResolveAtStep` (integration) asserts a workflow input carries only a delegation token/handle (no raw caller token), and that ai resolves it at tool-exec time to the caller's authority for the chokepoint write. *Final H1–H17 elaboration + exact contract tag/versions are pinned at pack certification (ENG-2097); this story is dispatch-blocked until that tag exists.* [ASSUMPTION: delegation-token shape — pinned by the contracts delta / identity mint surface.]
