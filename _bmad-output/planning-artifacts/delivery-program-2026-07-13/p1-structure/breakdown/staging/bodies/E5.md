## 🎯 Feature area

The apply stage is where accepted Proposals become curated wiki content — mechanically and safely. The apply engine writes exclusively through the `orvex-wiki-api` chokepoint with CAS `ifVersion`, per-page block ops, per-item receipts, and a per-(tenant,page) lease; ChangeSet atomicity is a workflow-state gate, never cross-store ACID, so a partial failure **pauses** with an explicit applied-vs-pending state (FR-STG13). Beautification happens at **triage**, before review — the rendered diff the reviewer approves IS the final content, stored as an immutable sha256-addressed artifact, keeping LLM latency out of the apply SLO (FR-STG14). Accepted ChangeSets can also be scheduled to apply at a future instant with conflict re-check at fire time (FR-STG28).

Why it exists: UJ-2's climax is ~100 documents updated with ~15 minutes of human attention; a paused mid-apply ChangeSet means a partially-mutated live wiki and must force an explicit human resume/abort — never silent partial success (PRD §4.2, §7). Durable orchestration lives centrally in `orvex-studio-workflows` calling this service's idempotent `/internal/*` step-APIs (NFR-STG3). [Source: mOzNDhn322 §4.2/§4.6; GOacy9kdJz AD-4/AD-5/AD-13]
