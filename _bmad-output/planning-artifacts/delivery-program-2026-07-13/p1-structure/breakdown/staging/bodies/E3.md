## ЁЯОп Feature area

The Librarian's triage stage is the differentiated heart of Orvex Studio's knowledge story: it turns raw Proposals into a curated wiki. For every Proposal it produces a Disposition + target route + routing-confidence score + rendered diff + one-line justification (FR-STG7), routes find-before-create against the living tree and the knowledge dedup surface (FR-STG8), and detects hard conflicts against the authoritative content path тАФ never a silent three-way prose merge (FR-STG9). This epic builds the `triage`, `routing` domain packages and the `cmd/triage` worker; LLM calls delegate to `orvex-studio-ai`, dedup candidates to `orvex-studio-knowledge`.

Why it exists: no profiled competitor ships an AI reviewer that merges, and only one stages AI writes at all (PRD ┬з1). The find-before-create rule ("never a duplicate sibling") and the no-auto-merge invariant are the product's defensible core. [Source: mOzNDhn322 ┬з4.2; GOacy9kdJz AD-3/AD-6]
