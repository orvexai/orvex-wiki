## 🎯 Feature area

Autonomy is earned, scoped, and reversible. This epic builds the `policy` domain package — the server-side verdict engine that decides how far the Librarian may act — plus the human review surface. The Autonomy Dial (`recommend`→`auto-apply-low-risk`→`full-auto`, all three in v1) is per-tenant + per-space policy, never prompt text (FR-STG10); Trust Tiers derive per-producer standing from acceptance history and gate auto-apply eligibility (FR-STG11); the Review Queue offers filtered bulk decide with a **mandatory per-item adjudication lane** for low-confidence routes so a confident mis-route cannot hide inside a 2-second bulk skim (FR-STG12).

Why it exists: UJ-2 (Marta clears 100 proposals over coffee) and UJ-3 (Priya turns the dial up and the Librarian earns it) are the human-attention payoff of the whole product. The dial being a server verdict — not a surface or prompt decision — is what makes destructive-op gating and SM-C3's "zero unreviewed grounding" structurally true.
