## 🎯 Feature area

Beyond intake, the Librarian "will maintain the wiki as well, keep it tidy" (Daniel). This epic builds the `sweep` domain package and `cmd/sweep` executor: a standing tidy pass whose findings become recommendations in the **same** Review Queue, governed by the **same** dial. Duplicate/merge detection with whole-doc supersession (FR-STG18), Guru-style staleness + verification queue with knowledge-side retrieval trust-gating (FR-STG19), structure & link hygiene (FR-STG20), a beautification sweep against the FR-STG14 mechanical bar (FR-STG21), and the AI-groundability flag + review-state gate that breaks the AI-writes→AI-grounds→AI-writes amplification loop (FR-STG22). Sweep schedules live centrally in `orvex-studio-workflows`; the compute lives here.

Why it exists: UJ-4 — Marta reviews the overnight tidy sweep in the queue she already works. SM-5 (declining duplicate rate) and SM-C3 (zero grounding on auto-applied-but-unreviewed content) are the mandate's proof. Sweeps default to agent-authored pages only; the human edit path stays untouched (§2.2). [Source: mOzNDhn322 §4.4 FR-STG18-22; GOacy9kdJz AD-6/AD-7]
