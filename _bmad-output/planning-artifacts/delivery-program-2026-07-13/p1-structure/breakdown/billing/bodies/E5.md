## 🗺️ Feature area

The **standard free month** — the trial (FR-68, D-B7 as amended by the 2026-07-13 PO ruling: **no card required**). This epic owns the trial as a **temporary billing entitlement** (`source=trial`, no Stripe subscription during the trial, billing's own one-month timer drives expiry), the **once-per-verified-email dedupe** (peppered HMAC in `trial_grants`), the **trial fair-use throttle** (100/wk advisory → 150 throttle, computed from Redis counters, consumers enforce), and the **launch-day retroactive backfill** (every pre-paywall account offered the same free month). Trial lifecycle is central Temporal sagas in orvex-studio-workflows calling billing step APIs (D-WF-1) — billing runs no workflow worker. Lapse = normal over-quota rules, nothing deleted (G5); starter/demo content (3–5 pages) counts against quota.

Governing canon: FR-B13/B14/B15/B16, A-TRIAL, D-B7 (card-free, 2026-07-13 ruling — supersedes D-S23), D-S17 (grant to user tenant, no personal-org mint).
