## 🗺️ Feature area

Metering is **finance, never enforcement** (A-METER). This epic owns `cmd/metering` — the scale-to-zero Trigger-fed worker that consumes `ai.usage.recorded` into an **append-only Postgres usage journal** (range-partitioned, DROP-PARTITION retention — Mongo struck, D-S12) plus **Redis fast counters** keyed **cost-center = call-site × principal** (D-B9, Studio's ADR-0002 call-site registry is the metering unit). It also owns the **reporting surface** (`GET /v1/usage/...` for console, in-app billing state, and Beta's trial-end summary) and the **reconciliation drift net** (subscription sweep vs Stripe API, cap re-assert, journal-vs-counter checksum — the correctness net for every webhook-gap class, NFR-B5). `ai.cap.*` events are consumed for **reporting only** — they never flip an entitlement or push a cap.

Governing canon: FR-B18/B19/B20/B21, A-METER, D-B9, D-S12, NFR-B5.
