## 🗺️ Feature area

This is billing's reason to exist: **one entitlement truth** (G1, FR-66). It owns the **append-only, versioned, polymorphic-principal entitlement record** (A-ENT — the one un-retrofittable property, lands in migration 001), the **single check primitive** `GET /v1/entitlements/{principal_type}/{principal_id}` (A-CHECK), the **shared caching client** with fail-consistent semantics (`orvex-studio-lib/entitlements` for satellites, a conforming TS client inside the AGPL engine — the license boundary cuts both ways), and the **contracts-owned gated-feature-key enum** every consumer enforces at its own edge. There is no second entitlement store anywhere in the family; client hiding is cosmetic only.

Governing canon: FR-B4/B5/B6, A-ENT, A-CHECK, D-B2/D-S17 (polymorphic user|org, free = personal Clerk account, NO org), G3 (never on hot path), NFR-B1/B4.
