## 🗺️ Feature area

The plan catalog is billing's public face and the seam every pricing surface renders from. It owns the **versioned plan-catalog data** (`free`, `personal` £7 monthly / £70 annual, `teams` display-only teaser, hidden `enterprise`), the **Stripe Product/Price object model** referenced exclusively by versioned `lookup_key`s, and the **VAT-inclusive GBP-only** display rules. This is the greenfield authored here for the first time — no wiki page previously defined a Price scheme (PRD §1: pnpm-lock grep = 0). It exists because the money path (E4) and the entitlement record (E2) both key off the plan-version a grant references (G4 grandfathering, A-ENT), and because the pricing page is a product surface (orvex-studio-ui) that must render one canonical catalog with one framing everywhere.

Governing canon: FR-B1/B2/B3/B3a, A-PRICE, D-B8 (lookup-key-only), D-S19 (GBP-only launch), R3 (repackaging-backlash anti-pattern).
