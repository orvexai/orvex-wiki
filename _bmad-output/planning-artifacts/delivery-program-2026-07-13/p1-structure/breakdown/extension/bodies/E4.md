## 🎯 Feature area

Delivery must never be silent. This epic is the **firewall + per-use consent gate** that wraps every injection: private memories require an explicit per-use opt-in before they enter a third-party prompt (FR-D4), the personal↔employer wall holds at the delivery boundary (FR-D4), injection only ever targets the user's own compose surface (FR-D5), and the extension captures nothing beyond that surface and exfiltrates no secrets (FR-D5, security first-class). The gate sits structurally *between* the composed-prompt payload and the E3 adapter injection call — no adapter can inject around it.
