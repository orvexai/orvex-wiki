As a **user on a provider that blocks injection** I want **delivery to degrade to a clean copy/paste hand-off of the enriched prompt** so that **I still get my Memory-enriched prompt even when a provider makes in-page injection impossible, and Orvex never breaches ToS to force it (FR-D5)**.
